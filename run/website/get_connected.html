Anyone is welcome to join EVIX. to start the process, please review our rules: FAQ and then fill out the contact form below (unless noted, all fields are required):

Please note, only local connections are supported at our FRA and VAN POPs. If you wish to connect by a tunnel, please select a pop from the locations list. If you are connecting via a VM interface (such as ones offered by iFog or Free Range Cloud), please select the local connection type!

Once your application has been received, it will be evaluated and processed by one of our volunteer staff. When your application has been approved, you will be contacted by email to establish a peering session. Please whitelist 'helpdesk@evix.org' in your mail client to avoid missing these important instructions.
